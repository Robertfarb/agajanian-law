Career Opportunities

AMWT&C recognizes that excellent attorneys and staff make a great law firm and we are always looking for passionate and dedicated individuals to join our team. We understand litigation is a highly energized and competitive industry and are looking for individuals who are able to meet the demands of the job, while maintaining a healthy work-life balance. Our firm values the importance of stress management and provides benefits that reflect our commitment to the health of our team. We are committed to our employees and invest in their advancement for success. If you are interested in joining our team, please forward us a copy of your resume to: phil@agajanianlaw.com or through the messaging function on our “Contact Us” page.
