William D. Anthony

Bill is a member of the General Liability Group and handles all aspects of litigation on a significant portion of the firm’s cases involving sports, leisure, recreation and motorsports litigation. He focuses on general and premises liability, government entity liability, insurance coverage, and commercial transactions. He is skilled in motion and appellate practice.

Bill is licensed in California and North Carolina.
