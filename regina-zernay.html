Regina S. Zernay

As a member of the Medical Malpractice Group, Regina participates in the defense of medical facilities and their staff. In her work with the General Liability Group, Regina assists in litigating motorsports matters.

Regina graduated cum laude from Chapman University School of Law with an emphasis in Entertainment Law. During law school, Regina served as the Production Editor for Chapman Law Review. She was a recipient of the National Order of Scribes Award. Prior to joining AMWT&C, Regina's work focused on construction defect, habitability, copyright, and business law.
